About

All the material related to this project are accessible at the Github repository

Future developments

The OM&TA ontologies at its status describes the very core relations between the main elements needed to represent techniques and materials in art. Future developments will include the use of the skos vocabularies in order to standardize and provide an overview on the techniques and material.
